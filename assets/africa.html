Trips: Tanzania and Zanzibar

June 2022

During the summer of 2022 my mom and I took a trip to Africa. The trip was 3 weeks long, and consisted of several safaris followed by a 5 day stay on the island state of Zanzibar. Tanzania and Zanzibar are south of the equator, so this was during their winter, although it is referred to just as the Dry Season. It was all booked off of the Signature Safari from Thomson Safaris, with the added Ruaha, Selous, and Zanzibar extensions.

The trip was incredible, I ended up taking about 10,000 pictures during the time we were there. Take a look at some of my favorites! All of these pictures except for one were taken with my phone. The pictures with a circular black vignette were taken by mounting my phone to binoculars with a $20 attachment from Amazon, the rest were with just my phone.
